Instagram

The daily app we use to view pictures on the fly.

What Is It?

Instagram is a photo and video-based social media platform that emerged in 2010 and has been a staple in terms of the digital social atmosphere. When it was first created the app served as a digital space for users to showcase and share their pictures with their followers who had the opportunity to like and comment on pictures. Since then, the app has grown to include many other features such as video uploading, slideshows, 24-hour stories, direct messaging, and live streaming. It remains one of the most used Tools of social media with upwards of 1 billion active users as of June 2018.

Who Uses It?

Instagram as a platform allows users to curate their experience based on the content they post and the people and tags that they follow. A large portion of the users on Instagram are within the 18-34 age range. This age range is also responsible for many of the strides we have made as a digital society. Instagram is not really as popular with older generations; as is the case with most other social media. Regardless the people who use it have created a digital society where content can easily be shared and disbursed in a matter of moments. As a user, there are easy ways to find content that you like such as following influencers in whatever community you are apart of or even following the hashtags that are important to you.

How Does It Spread?

Instagram is the perfect place for meme creators to deliver content. As most memes take form as either pictures or videos a platform that specializes in that would make the perfect home for this kind of content to circulate through. Memes almost never originate in the place that you saw them. Instagram has a big role in meme circulation because there are tons of pages that are dedicated to memes and memes alone. There is a meme page on Instagram for any interest.

Want to see some funny stuff on IG?

Also, some of the tools that Instagram is equipped with, help circulate content instantly

  • Hashtags- A hashtag is a keyword preceded by the hash (#) symbol that associates whatever was posted with that tag. So, if a person posted a clever meme with the hashtag #funny, a person looking at the meme could access more content tagged funny by selecting that tag. Similarly, if a person were to search the tag then all posts with that tag would become available. This technology helps users find the content that they are interested in quickly.
  • Direct Messaging – Direct messaging is exactly what it sounds like. A service that allows users to send direct messages to each other without the contents of the messages ever being disclosed to any other followers. In terms of meme spreadability, direct messaging is also a way to share posts that you’ve come across with specified users.
