The Spreadable Meme

Memes are a form of sharable media that offers entertainment or satire on current popular cultural phenomena.
At least that's one definition...

Why Does This Website Exist?

The main objective of this website is to ask the question, how do memes become spreadable? We take into account the history of how human communication has involved memes as well as the history of some memes. Within this site, there are descriptions of what spreadability is, what channels spreadability occurs over and a "Meme Timeline", which is a little something we have included that elaborates on the history of memes in depth.

How Are Memes Used?

Memes are a type of communication through a form of image, text, or video. They are primarily used for humor or enjoyment, either making jokes about topics like current events, funny representations of relatable thoughts and desires, or even something as simple as a silly picture of a cute animal.
